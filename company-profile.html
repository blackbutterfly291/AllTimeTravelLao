Company Profile

20 Years of Establishing Our Identity
A leading one-stop air ticketing service company in Laos

Dear Valued Customers and Partners,

All Time Travel Services Sole Co., Ltd. was established in 2006, starting with only three members as a small air ticketing agent. Over nearly 20 years of establishment and development, the company has continuously improved and evolved. Today, we have become one of the leading air ticketing companies, providing services in both air ticketing and domestic and international travel.

In 2023, the vision and goal of All Time Travel Services is to become a leading company in air ticketing services, delivering the highest quality of service.

By working closely with and deeply understanding the needs of our customers and partners, we are committed to providing services that are convenient, fast, and highly efficient.

Today, All Time Travel Services has grown from its humble beginnings into a stable and successful organization. Our achievements are not only a source of pride, but also a strong motivation for our team to continue striving for excellence, reaching new heights, and expanding to the international level.

Ethical Statement

Integrity, transparency, and responsible business conduct.

All Time Travel Service Sole Co., Ltd. is committed to conducting its business with integrity and in compliance with ethical standards, including anti-corruption and anti-bribery practices. Neither the company nor its employees shall offer, pay, promise, or receive any bribe, kickback, or other improper payment that may result in unethical business practices affecting the Company, its partners, or customers. All Time Travel Service strives to engage in sustainable and transparent business practices, especially in bidding processes and contractual agreements, ensuring mutual benefit for all parties involved.

Business Success Journey

From modest beginnings to a trusted travel partner.
2006

Founded in Vientiane as a small air ticketing team of three, focused on dependable reservations and clear communication with travelers and airline partners.

2008

Broadened airline partnerships and repeat corporate accounts by standardizing booking workflows and follow-up support across domestic routes.

2010

Invested in training and tools so staff could handle higher booking volume while keeping turnaround times short during peak travel seasons.

2015

Top Agency Award 2015 given by Sabre Corporation to All Time Travel Services.

2016

Top Agent Award 2016 of Excellence Honoring. In recognition of our outstanding performance and contribution to Thailand Tourism Authority. Given by Thai Airways International, Area Lao PDR

2018

International Award at Red Stars Level 2018 given by AirAsia.

2019

All Time Travel Services has achieved the distinction of receiving dual awards at both the domestic and international levels. Outstaning Lao Young Entrepreneur Award 2019 given by Laos National Chamber of Commerce and Industry. Revenue Performance Award 2019 given by THAI - Amadeus Southeast Asia.

2021

All Time Travel Services has been named the recipient of the Outstanding Young Entrepreneur Award 2021 given by Laos National Chamber of Commerce and Industry. We are proud to have received the Outstanding Young Entrepreneur Award for the second time, securing this honor once again.

2023

This is our second time receiving dual awards at both the domestic and international levels again. Top Sales Performance Award 2023 given by Lao Airlines. Best Support Performance Award 2023 given by THAI - Amadeus Southeast Asia.

2024

Outstaning Lao Young Entrepreneur Award 2019 given by Laos National Chamber of Commerce and Industry.

2025

Our third time receiving dual awards at both the domestic and international levels. Outstaning Lao Young Entrepreneur Award 2025 given by Laos National Chamber of Commerce and Industry. Best contribution as Golden Agent by Vietnam Airlines.
